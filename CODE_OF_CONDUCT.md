# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the kruntimes community a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity,
sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Expected Behavior

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive community include:

- demonstrating empathy and respect;
- giving and accepting constructive technical feedback;
- focusing on what is best for the project and community;
- taking responsibility for mistakes and repairing harm.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, intimidation, personal attacks,
sexualized language or attention, publishing private information without
permission, and conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
professional setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these
standards. They may edit, remove, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other
contributions that do not follow this Code of Conduct, and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation.

Report abusive or otherwise unacceptable behavior privately to a maintainer
listed in [MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md). Reports will be handled as
confidentially as practical. Maintainers who are the subject of a report must
recuse themselves from handling it.
